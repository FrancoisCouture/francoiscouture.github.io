Welcome to my webpage!

My name is Francois Couture and I am proud to present to you my webpage!
I am a NSCC graduate in Computer Electronics and I have worked as a technician for the province of Nova Scotia. Currently, I am enrolled at Saint Mary's University set to major in Computing Science and Business Administration, graduating in 2023.
At the top of the site, you will find navigation widgets that will lead you to other pages about my skills, education and experience. This website also contains information about itself! I explain in brief how the website works and how the source code influence the pages. Everything I provide is free to use for anyone.

Scrolling down will hide the navigation bar. You can bring it back into view by clicking on the dropdown arrow or by returning to the top of the page.

To reach me, please click the blue "Contact me" button at the top of the webpage.

This website is supposed to represent who I am and what I do in the most interactive way possible. As of now, it only contains the few things I am capable of. As I grow older and more experienced, I want the website to grow alongside me by carrying more experiences and more skills. It becomes both a portfolio and a living diary. My dream is to make this place elaborate, intricate. It will carry HTML5 games I create for fun. It will host my videos that I want to share with the world. There will be pictures about what I was able to accomplish. More navigation buttons to better express who I am. I want it to encapsulate my life so that anyone seeing this can get to know me and witness how I grew as a person.
I am not perfect, but I try every day to get better. That's why I'm presenting my page and why I'm thanking you for being part of the journey.
